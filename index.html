    General Rules

    No over-using racism.

    Do not impersonate other players (particularly an admin).

    No hacks and/or Bhop scripts. The use of any will warrant a permanent ban.

    No mic/chat spamming or excessive yelling unless an admin says it's all right based on a popular vote.

    Listen to admins; They have Final say.

    No advertising.

    Zombie Mod Rules

    Do not destroy barricades intentionally. Do not abuse !block (e.g using it to get people stuck inside or outside of a certain place). Do not Edge (e.g standing on the Edge of a spot close to the humans in order to get infected then infect them). Do not Disconnect/Suicide/Switch teams when you're the only Zombie left. Admins Please use discretion and allow time for new players to learn the rules. Punishments can include warnings, slay, kick, ban (not necessarily in that order). If you are punished by an admin, take a deep breath and try to understand what you did before you complain. To talk directly to admins, use team chat and type @ before your sentence (i.e. @what's up admins?). Zombie Mod Information We are currently debugging the frequent server crashes.
